Scotland

Nemo me impune lasseci

Scotland is a European country located in the British Isles, it is the northernmost point of the British Isles and is part of the United Kingdom together with England, Wales and Northern Ireland.

Scotland is known for many things, like their culture, intriguing history, and maybe above all else their highly unique accent. Scotland is also a place of great natural beauty, see the Scottish Highlands as an example.
